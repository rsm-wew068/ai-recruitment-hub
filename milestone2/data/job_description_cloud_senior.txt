About the Role
We are seeking an experienced Senior Software Engineer specializing in Cloud Infrastructure to join our team, driving the design, development, and operation of large-scale cloud computing systems. As a Senior Software Engineer, you will be responsible for leading the development of cloud infrastructure technologies, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and ensuring the highest levels of system reliability, performance, and security. You will have the opportunity to work on complex problems, develop innovative solutions, and contribute to the growth and evolution of our cloud infrastructure.

Responsibilities
Design, develop, and deploy scalable, secure, and efficient cloud infrastructure systems, including architecture, provisioning, and management of cloud resources. Collaborate with development teams to ensure seamless integration of cloud infrastructure with applications and services. Participate in the evaluation, selection, and implementation of new cloud technologies, tools, and services. Lead and participate in the development of automation scripts, tools, and workflows to streamline cloud infrastructure management. Troubleshoot and resolve complex cloud infrastructure issues, working closely with other engineers, developers, and stakeholders. Develop and maintain technical documentation, including architecture diagrams, design documents, and operational guides. Mentor and guide junior engineers, providing technical guidance, code reviews, and best practices. Collaborate with security, compliance, and governance teams to ensure cloud infrastructure meets security, compliance, and regulatory requirements.

Required Skills
Minimum 6 years of experience in software development, with a focus on cloud infrastructure, including design, development, and operation of large-scale cloud computing systems. Proficiency in one or more programming languages, such as Java, Python, C++, or Ruby. Experience with cloud infrastructure platforms, including Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform, or similar technologies. Strong understanding of cloud computing concepts, including scalability, availability, security, and performance. Experience with infrastructure as code tools, such as Terraform, CloudFormation, or Azure Resource Manager. Strong problem-solving skills, with the ability to analyze complex issues, identify root causes, and develop effective solutions. Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams.

Preferred Qualifications
Experience with containerization technologies, such as Docker, Kubernetes, or similar platforms. Familiarity with agile development methodologies, including Scrum, Kanban, or similar frameworks. Experience with continuous integration and continuous deployment tools, such as Jenkins, GitLab CI/CD, or similar technologies. Strong understanding of networking fundamentals, including protocols, architectures, and security. Experience with monitoring, logging, and analytics tools, such as Prometheus, Grafana, or similar technologies. Participation in open-source projects or contributions to cloud infrastructure communities.
